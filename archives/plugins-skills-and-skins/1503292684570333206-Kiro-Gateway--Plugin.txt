    **Bring your Kiro Pro models in your Hermes model picker natively, including Opus and Sonnet. **
    
    Requires a Kiro Plan with Google or GitHub login.
    -# As of right now, I haven't gotten Builder ID / Enterprise sessions to work properly.
    
    Made this plugin since, as of writing, they are offering a 1-month trial for their Pro subscription, including pretty solid limits for Claude models.
    
    **## install
    Inside your Hermes environment, run:**
    ```curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/TabooHarmony/hermes-kiro-plugin/master/install.sh | bash```
    
    **Then, install kiro-cli, and log in.**
    ```curl -fsSL https://cli.kiro.dev/install | bash
    kiro-cli login --use-device-flow```
    
    After that, you can now select and use Kiro models from `hermes model`.
