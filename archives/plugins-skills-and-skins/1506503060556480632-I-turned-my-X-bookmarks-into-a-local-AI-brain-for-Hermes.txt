    You save great posts on X.
    
    Launch ideas. Growth tactics. AI agent examples. Product insights. Market signals. Then they disappear into the bookmark graveyard.
    
    So I built **Awaek** — a Hermes skill that turns your saved X bookmarks into local AI context.
    
    Not just bookmark search. The point is action: Hermes can use what you already saved to help you:
    
    - find market signals
    - generate content angles
    - write posts from your own taste
    - plan launches
    - surface product ideas
    - decide what to build next
    
    Your bookmarks become source material for your agent.
    
    It runs locally with SQLite + `xurl`, and your saved-post library stays on your machine.
    
    Repo:
    https://github.com/1lystore/awaek
    
    Would love feedback from Hermes users:
    
    1. Is the positioning clear?
    2. What would you want Hermes to do with your saved posts?
    3. Where does onboarding feel confusing?
