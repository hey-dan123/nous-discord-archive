    me and my friend made a plugin called OP because all SMS providers are just Twilio wrappers that have terrible free tiers, and make you sign up as a business in order to send texts.
    
    instead of filling out a 10DLC form on twilio, with OP you can start sending texts to yourself from Hermes in two commands.
    
    you can also use OP to receive 2FA codes for creating accounts for your Hermes.
    
    all you need to do is run
    
    `hermes plugins install JoshMayerr/hermes-op-plugin --enable`
    
    then 
    
    `hermes op setup --phone +14155551234 --webhook-url https://your-tunnel.trycloudflare.com/webhooks/op`
    
    and your agent can start texting you from any phone!
    
    link to plugin: https://github.com/JoshMayerr/hermes-op-plugin
    
    you dont even need an api key to start, the plugin will do it all for you
