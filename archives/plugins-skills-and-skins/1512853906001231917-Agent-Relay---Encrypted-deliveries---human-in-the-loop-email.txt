    Two things in one channel between your agents and you:
    
    1. Deliveries — agents send reports, HTML, PDFs, images, and other artifacts to a private inbox you review in the browser.
    2. Email Review Relay — agents submit encrypted outbound email drafts; you preview and approve or reject before anything sends.
    
    **Everything is end-to-end encrypted** (decrypted in your browser, not on the server). Hosted at https://arelay.app or self-host from https://github.com/mmmikael/arelay (MIT).
    
    Hermes setup:
    
    `hermes skills tap add mmmikael/arelay-skills
    hermes skills install mmmikael/arelay-skills/agent-relay-api
    hermes skills install mmmikael/arelay-skills/agent-relay-e2ee`
    
    Set `AGENT_RELAY_URL=https://arelay.app` and an agent token from the portal. 
    Skills repo: https://github.com/mmmikael/arelay-skills
    
    Happy to answer questions or walk through wiring it up.
