    time-gap stays silent during active conversations, but when you return after a meaningful gap—or cross into a new calendar day—it privately reminds Hermes that earlier context may now be stale.
    
    No timestamps on every message, no transcript pollution, no in-memory state. It reads the last assistant timestamp from `state.db` in read-only mode and fails open on errors.
    
    Just released `v1.0.0`—feedback is very welcome! 🚀
