    🚀 **WhatsApp Group Summary Bot** — Now on npm!
    
    `npx whatsapp-group-summary`
    
    Problem: Hermes WhatsApp only records messages from whitelisted users or @mentions. Group chat from 100+ members? Completely invisible.
    
    Solution:
    • Record ALL group messages silently to JSONL
    • Zero token cost for regular chat
    • Bot only responds when @mentioned
    • Auto-summary 2x daily (07:00 & 23:00 WIB)
    • Clean WhatsApp-friendly format, casual tone
    • Optional 🔥 Roast Mode (sarcastic summaries)
    
    Architecture: Bridge (Node.js) → Adapter (Python) → JSONL Logs → Cron Jobs → Summary
    
    Install: `npx whatsapp-group-summary`
