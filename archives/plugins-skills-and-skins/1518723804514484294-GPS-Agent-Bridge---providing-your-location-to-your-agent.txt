[2026-06-26T00:22:45.752000+00:00] madvulcan (id=1519860436705480899)
    Gps-agent-bridge has had a bunch of updates and optimizations. Big news: we now have an Android app specifically designed to work with the GPS Agent Bridge that uses much less battery than the app it defaulted to before (gpsdRelay). 
    
    The old app kept pinging location updates constantly, even if you were stationary at home. The new app, GPS Agentbridge Android, has configurable fencing and default timing for updating your location. If you're stationary, it doesn't send updates to the receiving agent constantly - just at whatever frequency you specify. But if you're on the move, it starts updating your agent with your new location. You can specify how far you need to move before it starts updating your location - default is 500 meters before it starts updating your agent's server with your updated location. 
    
    This should decrease battery impact substantially.
    
    There are two Android APKs available - one uses Google Play services to increase accuracy and further improve battery life. The other is for people who don't want Google Play Services on their phone, and uses only the phone's hardware, at a slight accuracy cost (indoors specially, if GPS is unavailable) and uses slightly more battery.

[2026-06-26T19:31:17.948000+00:00] madvulcan (id=1520149475404808313)
    Big battery-saving update to the Android app - deep sleep mode.  GPS is turned completely off when stationary, and the phone's accelerometer is is armed instead. This sensor costs less than 0.01% battery per hour and fires the instant the phone is physically moved (picked up, walked with, car door closes). GPS then snaps back to active 30s polling. Overnight, this means near-zero battery drain — the phone only checks GPS when you actually pick it up.
    
    The app will still send location updates to the agent/server at the max interval specified by the user (default every 10 minutes), but with these updates, that interval can be turned up higher, saving more battery life (less waking up and polling for GPS data and sending it if the phone hasn't moved).
