    Hey all, sharing a skill I built for running actual multi-hour research runs without the model quitting early or losing track of time.
    
    **The problem:** you say "research X for 4 hours" and the LLM either quits in 10 minutes or rambles forever. It has no clock.
    
    **The fix:** this skill moves the clock out of the model. it uses Hermes cron — every 15 min a fresh tick runs, checks the real wall-clock, does ONE focused research step, saves to disk, and exits. Only the actual stop_time ends the run.
    
    **What you get back**:
    
    - a structured report with an executive summary
    - every claim cited to a source
    - a quality self-grade at the end
    - crash-proof : any tick can die, next one picks up
    
    **install**: hermes skills tap add Kaushal-Panchal/hermes-long-research hermes skills install long-research
    
    **usage**: "research for 4h", fill in the goal file, say "start", walk away.
    
    **repo**: https://github.com/Kaushal-Panchal/hermes-long-research
    
    still iterating on it!  Feedback welcome.
