    We just shipped cross-machine skill inventory: an opt-in, read-only view of the agent skills installed across every machine and harness. We're looking for more harness operators, people responsible for the skills, rules, MCPs, their versions, and maintenance. As a builder or as team, reach out for a partner code for free access. Before we open this up broadly, we want a small group of real multi-machine users to run it for a week or two and tell us what breaks or feels off. A number of teams in regulatory/compliance environments can't build out their harnesses until this level of audit for each tool is in place. This issue is the sign-up sheet: https://github.com/smith-horn/skillsmith/issues/1707. Thanks in advance!
