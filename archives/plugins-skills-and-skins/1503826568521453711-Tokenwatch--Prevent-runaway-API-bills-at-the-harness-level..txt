    I may or may not have woken up to a surprise API bill as I was on a hermes version with kanban but before failure tracking was native, so my agent ran up a huge amount of cached and input tokens. 
    
    This plugin is designed to let you set custom limits and display warnings in your home channel or custom channel. 
    
    It's a little brittle for my liking as it relies on the same path as /stop command, but hopefully that will get fixed with a paired PR and it'll instead use pre_api_request hook as the entry point. Install, set a limit, your warning message numbers, and then it'll either just warn you, or interrupt sessions until you reset limits or unfreeze. 
    
    Please let me know what you think! I'll update this if my PR gets approved.
