    Ever left Hermes running a long task in the background and kept switching tabs just to check if it finished? Or forgot about it entirely and left it waiting for your input?
    
    I built a simple macOS notification plugin to fix this: macos-notify
    It sends a system alert the moment Hermes finishes. Clicking the notification brings your terminal (Ghostty, iTerm2, Warp, etc.) back to the foreground.
    
      Install:
      hermes plugins install konyu/hermes-macos-notify
      hermes plugins enable macos-notify
    
      👉 https://github.com/konyu/hermes-macos-notify
