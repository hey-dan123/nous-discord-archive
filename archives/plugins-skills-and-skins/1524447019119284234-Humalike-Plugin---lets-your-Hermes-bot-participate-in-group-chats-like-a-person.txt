    Bots break in group chats: they answer every message, instantly, all the time. This plugin gives your Hermes bot the social sense to participate like a person. It knows when to jump in, when to stay quiet, and how to sound like a member of the group.
    
    What it does:
    - Turn-taking: decides when to reply vs stay silent, and splits responses into natural, human-paced messages instead of one wall of bot text
    - Persona: /soul enhance turns a one-line description into a full character with a real voice
    - Theory of mind: considers how a message will land for the reader before sending it
    - Social learning: picks up your group's slang, formatting, and jokes, so the bot sounds like a member, not a visitor
    
    Setup is one clone + enable; the plugin auto-configures itself on first start. Powered by the Humalike APIs.
    
    🔗 https://github.com/Humalike/hermes-humalike-plugin
