    I used the council to explore the questions around the antiquated 40 hour work week, looking for a single answer that would be a good replacement. The council helped me to appreciate that part of why the 40 hour work week is so bad was that it was a specific solution to a specific vocation at a specific point in history, so replacing it with just one thing would be as bad as just keeping it. 
    
    It ended up being the inspiration for a blog post exploring solutioning the work schedule around the actual nature of a worker's role.
