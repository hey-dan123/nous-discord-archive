    OddCor Collectibles — Hermes × NVIDIA × Stripe Hackathon Submission
        
        OddCor already existed as my collector-run business. The hackathon build was the operating framework around it: Hermes Agent orchestration, NVIDIA Nemotron 3 Ultra for pricing reasoning, NemoClaw/OpenShell for sandboxed worker lanes, and live Stripe Checkout with human approval gates.
        
       Agents propose, operator controls what goes live. The storefront (https://oddcor-collectibles.vercel.app) shows only priced, approved inventory. The operator console is Discord-auth protected. Stripe handles payments; payment/shipping details stay in Stripe. NemoClaw/OpenShell keeps worker lanes sandboxed and away from Stripe secrets.
        
        Real small-business use case: idle graded/raw card inventory → agent prices with market comps → operator approves/challenges/holds → live Stripe checkout → webhook confirms → fulfillment queue.
