    If you use Chinese LLM providers (MiniMax, DeepSeek, GLM, Qwen), you've probably noticed Chinese characters leaking into output mid-sentence. It's a known quirk from CJK-heavy training data.
    
    This plugin hooks into Hermes' transform_llm_output pipeline and strips those characters automatically. Korean and Japanese are preserved by design — it only targets Chinese ideographs and Cyrillic (the scripts that tend to glitch).
    
    Zero config. Install and forget:
    `hermes plugins install evanyudis/cjk_sanitizer --enable
    hermes gateway restart`
    
    MIT, open, 30 lines of Python: https://github.com/evanyudis/cjk_sanitizer
