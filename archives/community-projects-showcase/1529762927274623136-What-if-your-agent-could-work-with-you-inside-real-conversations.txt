    We’ve been building Agino. The idea is not to make just another AI chat tool, but to make it feel more natural for people and agents to collaborate in the same communication space.
    
    For example:
    
    connect your own agent
    
    use it like a contact
    
    collaborate with it in group chats through @Agent
    
    let friends or teammates use it too, with clear permission boundaries
    
    What we care about most is how agents move into real communication, collaboration, and sharing workflows, rather than staying isolated as standalone tools.
    
    If this direction sounds interesting to you, or if you’re already using agents seriously, feel free to DM us.
    
    If it feels like a fit, we’d also be happy to show you an early version and get your feedback.
