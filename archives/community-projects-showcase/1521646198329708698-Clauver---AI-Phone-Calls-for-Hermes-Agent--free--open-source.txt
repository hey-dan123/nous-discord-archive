    I lost my voice for 2 months — thought it was cancer. Couldn't even book a doctor appointment by phone. Every AI voice tool I found was expensive. So I built my own to replace my voice, free and open source, and now it works with Hermes.
    
    What it does: Your Hermes makes real phone calls. Full two-way conversation — speaks, listens, responds. Uses your existing LLM/TTS/STT. You only pay phone minutes (~$0.013/min).
    
    Free TTS (Edge) + Free STT (local Whisper) — zero extra AI cost. Same providers you already have in your Hermes config.
    
    Just tell Hermes:
      • "Call my doctor and book tomorrow at 2pm"
      • "Call me at 7am with my top 3 priorities"
      • "Call me when you're done with a brief report"
      • "Call this lead and ask if Thursday works for a meeting"
    
    Setup: skill install → run setup script → run provision script (auto-connects Twilio + LiveKit) → done in 5 min.
    
    I'm recovering now (no cancer thankfully) 😊 and I want this to help others — anyone who can't speak, has phone anxiety, or wants Hermes to handle phone tasks for them.
    
     Contributors welcome: inbound call handler, structured results to chat, call recordings.
    
      ⭐ https://github.com/mustafa-ramax/clauver-voice-hermes
