    Sharing a Hermes user story / use case I just published on X:
    https://x.com/tborges77/status/2070231771174588825?s=20
    
    I use Hermes as a local-first research desk. Scheduled jobs watch source streams, capture evidence, and write into a living KB with entities, timelines, backlinks, and source references.
    
    The useful part is not a one-off summary. Hermes asks what changed, what connects to older threads, and what deserves a deeper pass.
    
    Parallax is my preferred consumption layer: a private podcast-style format where two or three AI hosts/guests discuss a research thread, so I can listen while walking, driving, or away from a screen.
    
    The loop is:
    sources → extraction → local KB → synthesis → private Parallax podcast → personal listening
    
    This changed the workflow from “remember to check everything” into “let the pipeline surface what changed and why it matters.”
