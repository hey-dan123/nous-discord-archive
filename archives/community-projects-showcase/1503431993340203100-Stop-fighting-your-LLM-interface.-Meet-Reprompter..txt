    Hey everyone! 
    
    Build my first project using Hermes.
    
    I’ve spent way too many hours in "prompting purgatory"—manually re-typing instructions just to get ChatGPT or Claude to stop being generic.
    
    I built Reprompter to kill that friction. It’s a native Chrome extension (we call it the Reprompter Pill) that sits directly inside your favorite LLM UIs.
    
    The TL;DR:
    
    Speed: Refines your intent in 1.25 seconds flat.
    
    Workflow: No tab switching. It lives inside ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini.
    
    Focus: It’s designed to eliminate the "re-prompting tax" that eats up your focus. 
    
    Love to hear your feedback. Thanks for looking.
