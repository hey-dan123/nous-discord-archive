    Hey everyone,
    
    I love the Hermes ecosystem, but constantly switching to the terminal and writing repetitive prompts every time I needed to read a schematic, check a PDF, or analyze an image was slowing me down. I wanted a completely frictionless way to interact with my local agent.
    
    So, I built Hermes Drop. It’s a minimalist, desktop dropzone for Hermes.
    
    What it does:
    
    Zero Prompting: You don't type anything. Just drag a file (PDF, code, image) and drop it on the UI. The app detects the file type, formats the perfect command, and triggers Hermes in the background.
    
    100% Local & Lightweight: Built with Python and CustomTkinter. No bloated web servers, no heavy frameworks.
    
    Zero Friction: Just a clean, dark-mode window that sits on your desktop, ready to process your files instantly.
    
    It's completely open-source. I built this mainly to solve my own workflow issues and learn, but I'd love to hear your feedback or feature ideas!
