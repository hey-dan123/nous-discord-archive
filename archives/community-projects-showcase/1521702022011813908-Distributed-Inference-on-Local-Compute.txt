    Hermes now runs on Koretex 🐳
    
    Koretex is a distributed inference network, a pool of independent consumer machines serving open models instead of a centralized datacenter. 
    
    We just wired it into Hermes, so you can point your agent at the network and run on it directly.
    
    And it's live, not a mockup: here's Hermes serving NVIDIA's Nemotron 3 Nano 30B through Koretex right now, a real agent running on distributed community compute.
    
    Your Hermes agent draws on a network of open models, not a single machine or a metered cloud API.
    
    The people supplying the compute earn for it, instead of the value flowing to a datacenter.
    
    Private, open, sovereign, the same ethos this community already runs on.
    
    We're in early alpha and Hermes users are exactly who we want testing it. Everyone who joins gets 1,000,000 free credits to run real workloads on the network, plus a direct line to me and real say in where it goes. No referral games, just early access and honest feedback.
