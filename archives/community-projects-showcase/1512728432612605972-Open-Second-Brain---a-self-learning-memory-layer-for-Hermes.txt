    Open Second Brain gives your agent a memory it doesn't lose: everything it learns from what you do is stored as plain Markdown in your Obsidian vault - readable, editable, yours. It watches your corrections, turns them into preferences, reviews them in a nightly "dream" pass, and retires rules that stop being true.
    
    It plugs into Hermes natively, following the official memory-provider documentation - one line in the config, and Hermes shares the same brain with every agent you run, on every machine: a preference written on your VPS gets applied on your laptop.
