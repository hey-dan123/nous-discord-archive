    Hermes Eats World — hand Hermes any running Windows application and it wraps around it, ingests it, and operates it under your direction. Instead of screenshot-and-click theater, it perceives the app as structure: walk the UIA tree, classify a tier (native API → accessibility → vision fallback), act through real control patterns, and verify against a pre/post snapshot diff. Runs as a Python WebSocket sidecar Hermes calls as a tool, with every action routed through Interceptor and logged to OathLedger. Phase 0 spike is proven — find_window, UWP frame drilling, thread-timeout UIA, DPI-correct capture. It was built first for a partner whose hands don’t cooperate the way they used to.
