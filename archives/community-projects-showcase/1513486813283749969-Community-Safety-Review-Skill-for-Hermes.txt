    I started a new Hermes workflow skill focused on community safety review.
    
    The goal is to help communities review suspicious claims, unsafe links, impersonation signs, unclear ownership, missing verification sources, and misleading messages in a structured way.
    
    Phase 1 is intentionally small and easy to review.
    
    Current version includes:
    - Hermes workflow skill foundation
    - safety review template
    - suspicious signal checklist
    - LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH / UNKNOWN risk level framework
    - moderator handoff notes
    - neutral output style
    - human review disclaimer
    
    Important note:
    This skill does not automatically accuse anyone.
    It does not make enforcement decisions.
    It only creates neutral review notes for humans to check.
