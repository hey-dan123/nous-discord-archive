    Quick update on my second Hermes skill: Community Safety Review.
    
    I opened a PR for Phase 2:
    https://github.com/NousResearch/hermes-agent/pull/44280
    
    This skill generates a neutral Markdown safety review report from public, reviewer-supplied project information.
    
    It does not label any project as a scam or make final accusations. Instead, it highlights public risk signals, positive trust signals, and missing information so a human reviewer can make a better decision.
    
    Current report includes:
    
    * Review level: LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH / HUMAN REVIEW REQUIRED
    * Detected risk signals with evidence
    * Positive trust signals
    * Missing information
    * Public-signals-only note
    * Manual verification required disclaimer
    
    Public information only. No automated enforcement. Manual verification required.
