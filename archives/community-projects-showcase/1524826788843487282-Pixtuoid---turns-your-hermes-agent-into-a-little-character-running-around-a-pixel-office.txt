    hermes agent kinda lives invisibly in your chats — you talk to it but never actually see it doing anything.
    
    so i made it visible. it's a little terminal app that draws a pixel-art office, and your hermes agent shows up as a little dude who walks in, grabs a desk, and starts typing whenever it's working a turn — then gets up and wanders around when it's idle. so you can literally watch it work.
    
    and if you've got other coding agents running too — claude code, codex, cursor, whatever — each one shows up as its own character at a nearby desk, so it's your whole agent crew sharing one tiny office.
    
    it wires in through hermes's hooks (~/.hermes/config.yaml) — approve it once in hermes and your agent's live.
