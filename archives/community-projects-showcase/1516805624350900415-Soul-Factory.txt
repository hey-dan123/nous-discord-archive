    ~4,800 loadable soul.md personality files for agents. Each soul.md has 9 sections (identity, philosophy, decision patterns, mental models, etc.). 
    
    Think "Wikipedia of minds". You grab one, drop it into your agent's personality layer (Hermes, Claude Code, Codex, whatever supports soul.md) ), and your agent has a coherent cognitive profile: identity, philosophy, how it makes decisions, communication style, contradictions and so on. 
    
      There's also a builder section where you can:
     - Forge a new soul from a name + source materials
     - Combine 2–5 existing souls into a blended mind
     - Download you new soul and start using it, or tweak it to your liking
