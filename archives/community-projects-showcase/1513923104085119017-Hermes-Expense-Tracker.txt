    I’ve turned Hermes into my family expense tracker! 
    
    Now I have a shared expense-tracking system powered by Hermes, with a centralized database and a dedicated Telegram bot for each family member (my wife and me, for now) to log expenses throughout the day.
    
    By the end of the day, Hermes can generate detailed reports with charts and insights. At the end of the month, I can even ask it to automatically create a full presentation summarizing our spending, trends, and categories.
    
    It’s been a surprisingly fun and practical use case for multi-agent profiles and shared data.
