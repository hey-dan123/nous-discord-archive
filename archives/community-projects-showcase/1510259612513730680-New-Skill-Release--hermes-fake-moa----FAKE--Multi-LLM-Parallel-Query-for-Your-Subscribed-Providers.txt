    I just released **hermes-fake-moa**, a skill that allows you to send the same prompt to multiple LLMs simultaneously using your own subscribed providers.
    
    📍 Repo: https://github.com/kgmkm/hermes-fake-moa
    
    **Mainly targets** subscription / private providers such as:
    - **OpenCode Go**
    - **Nous Portal**
    - **NVIDIA NIM**
    - and many others (currently supports 12 providers / 600+ models)
    
    ### Why I made it
    The built-in `moa` tool in Hermes Agent is unfortunately hardcoded to OpenRouter only. I accidentally used it and lost **$6 LUL**
    That sad moment became the birth of this skill.
    
    ### What it does
    - Automatically scans the providers you have registered in Hermes Agent
    - Shows you a clean list of available **Provider + Model** combinations
    - Recommends models while taking your subscriptions and pricing into account
    - Lets you select 2–5 models and fires the same prompt to all of them in parallel
    - Displays the responses side-by-side for easy human comparison
    
    ### Origin Story
    This skill started as a small subset of the skills I was developing for **Japanese novel writing and revision** in Hermes Agent.  
    Thanks to this tool, I can now get critiques from completely different LLMs than the one that wrote the draft. It dramatically improved my ability to catch setting contradictions and receive fresh, sensory-style feedback — resulting in noticeably higher novel quality.
