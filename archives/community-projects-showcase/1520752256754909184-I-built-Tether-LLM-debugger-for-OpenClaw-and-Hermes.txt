    Built a debugger for local AI agents called Tether.
    If your OpenClaw or Hermes agent ever did something unexpected and you had no idea where it went wrong, this is for that.
    Point your agent's base_url at Tether's proxy. It captures every LLM call and shows it as a node graph with time-travel replay, per-node cost, and latency. No SDK changes, no config.
    Fully local. Nothing leaves your machine. macOS and Linux.
    GitHub: ||github.com/Hqzdev/Tether||
    Site: ||tetherapp.vercel.app||
    
    If you're running multi-agent setups or custom skills, that's the exact use case I'm building for. Feedback welcome.
