    I built a self-hosted Honcho Dashboard to fit my own needs, OpenConcho is great and got me most of the way there, but I wanted something that matched my taste a bit more on aesthetics and filled a few functionality gaps. (shoutout nodaylight <3)
    
    It's a fast, privacy-first web UI for a self-hosted Honcho v3 server, built on Next.js + the official @honcho-ai/sdk. A few things it does:
    
    - Browse workspaces, peers, sessions, messages, and conclusions
    - Edit workspace & peer config (reasoning, peer cards, summaries, dreams) right from the UI
    - Semantic search over conclusions + schedule dreams / watch the reasoning queue
    - Webhooks management
    - An operator panel that reads your Postgres read-only — db size, pgvector status, throughput, a 52-week activity heatmap, log tail, and diagnostics
    - Multi-instance (point it at several Honcho servers), same-origin proxy with a server-side allowlist, one-command Docker deploy
    
    All open source, would love feedback, ideas, or contributions:
    https://github.com/outpoints/honcho-dashboard
