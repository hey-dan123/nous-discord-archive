    Built a fully native iPhone, Mac, and CLI chat client for Hermes — same conversation across every device, E2E encrypted, no account, free, open source.
    
    Think "iMessage for your AI agent."
    
    Action Button on iPhone: map it to chat4000 and one press from the lock screen jumps straight into chat — hold to dictate, release to send, or type instead. No unlock → find chat → tap → type.
    
    E2E-encrypted relay built in — no Tailscale, no port-forwarding to reach Hermes from anywhere. And you can run a regular VPN on your phone now — no more burning your VPN slot on Tailscale.
    
    Android, Windows, and Linux clients coming soon — waitlists open at https://chat4000.com/platforms
