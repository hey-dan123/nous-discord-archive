    Hey Hermes crew, founder of Koretex here.
    We're building a distributed inference network on Apple Silicon: pool idle Macs into a network that serves open models, so you own the compute instead of renting it from a cloud. It exposes an OpenAI-compatible endpoint, so you can point Hermes Agent straight at it and run your agent on a sovereign network of Macs.
    It's a clean fit for how a lot of us already run Hermes: local-first, open models, private by default, no vendor in the loop. The difference is you're not capped by a single machine, the network gives your agent more models and more headroom to draw on.
    We're opening early alpha, and fellow Hermes Mac users are exactly who we want in first. 
    Everyone who joins gets 1000 free credits to run real workloads on the network, plus a direct line to the team and a real say in what gets built. 
    
    Mission is simple: sovereign silicon for all.
