    Built a Hermes Agent plugin that lets you chat with AI directly inside Minecraft. Each player gets their own session, commands, tools, the whole deal. No server mods needed, just a Mineflayer bot (HermesBot) that joins your world, wanders around, and talks to you in chat. Type `$help` in-game and she responds. Tool progress goes via `/tell` private whisper so public chat stays clean. v0.0.1's out and running on my server.
