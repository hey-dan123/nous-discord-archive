    More of a concept at this point to inspire others but I've started building a solution to improve observability and debugging experience for Hermes enabled automations: https://github.com/patrickluvsoj/agentflow-health
    
    
    
    Some learnings that led to this ideas 👇
    #1 Longer running tasks require you to break them into sub-tasks. For instance I have a automation to research businesses which was broken up into
    - Source list of companies
    - Run research
    - Review by another model before publish
    
    To debug this, you need track the full pipeline but they lived as separate CRONs or Skills.
    
    #2 As you add more automations, keeping track of all of them and their inter-dependencies without visualization becomes difficult.
    i.e. Forgetting about a skill you created that defaults all research tasks to a specific tool/model that became stale
    
    #3 The scaffolding of stitching together CRONS, scripts and skills also helps the agent navigate your automations better
