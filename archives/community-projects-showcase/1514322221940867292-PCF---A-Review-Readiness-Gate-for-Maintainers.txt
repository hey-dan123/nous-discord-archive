    Built a small maintainer-readiness tool for issues/PRs:
    
    https://github.com/VrtxOmega/premature-contribution-firewall
    
    It is **not an AI detector**. It is a review-readiness gate.
    
    The idea is simple: before a maintainer spends time on a PR/issue, PCF checks whether it is scoped, reproducible, tested, evidence-backed, and worth human attention.
    
    One-command preflight:
    
    `npx premature-contribution-firewall preflight your-pr.json`
    
    Read-only by default. Zero dependencies. Includes a 69-case benchmark + adversarial red-team cases.
    
    Would genuinely love feedback from anyone maintaining repos, reviewing PRs, or dealing with noisy AI-generated contributions.
    The visuals weren’t just made up for the post.
    
    I used real PCF output and turned it into clearer shareable graphics so people could understand the pass/fail + repair flow quickly.
