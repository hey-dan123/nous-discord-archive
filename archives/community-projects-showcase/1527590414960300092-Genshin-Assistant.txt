    🗡️ Genshin Assistant — Your AI Companion for Teyvat
    Genshin Impact is a beautiful game with a crushing amount of systems to juggle — characters, weapons, artifacts, teams, domains, bosses, and a meta that never sits still. Genshin Assistant is the companion that takes all of that off your plate. It's an AI-powered assistant that lives with your account and helps you play smarter, build better, and enjoy the game instead of spreadsheeting it.
    
    🎯 Build characters around your playstyle
    Not every character needs to be built the "optimal" way — and with Genshin Assistant, they don't have to be. Tell it how you like to play, and it helps you customize any character to fit your style while still pushing them toward their real potential. From your first constellation to a fully min-maxed unit, it walks you through upgrading each character step by step so nothing gets left on the table.
    
    🤖 An assistant that actually teaches the game
    Attached to the app is a conversational AI that gets to know your roster. It explains what each character does, how their kit works, and why certain decisions matter — so you learn the game faster without grinding wikis. New to a character? Ask. Unsure why a team clicks? It'll break it down. The goal isn't just to give you answers, but to make you a better player.
